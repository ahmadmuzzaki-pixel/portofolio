01

PT Lokasaji Group · Oct 2024 – Jun 2025

Full Operational Turnaround of a Distressed FMCG Manufacturer

Cost Reduction Supply Chain HR Restructuring E-Commerce ERP Implementation
+

The Challenge

Joined a 30-person FMCG snack manufacturer with a critical cost structure — product COGS sitting at ~80% on key SKUs, no direct supplier relationships, no financial visibility, an oversized workforce, and two underperforming branches bleeding cash. The company had already reached the point of financial distress before I arrived.

What I Did

  • Identified outsourced production as the primary COGS driver; restructured best-seller SKUs to in-house manufacturing and sourced 3 new direct suppliers with 21-day payment terms — replacing expensive ad-hoc online procurement.
  • Redesigned outsourced labor compensation from flat daily rates to a minimum-output + performance bonus model, improving accountability and throughput without headcount changes.
  • Conducted KPI-based workforce analysis across all 30 roles, identified 3–4 redundant positions, presented a data-driven business case to ownership, and led approved restructuring execution.
  • Recommended and executed closure of 2 underperforming branches, redirecting resources to online frozen food sales and local bazaar events.
  • Implemented Accurate ERP system across the business — integrating production costs, inventory management, reorder points, payroll, and supplier/customer payment reconciliation for the first time.
  • Overhauled Shopee and Tokopedia storefronts: rebuilt product listings, trained admin team, implemented complaint resolution SOP, and introduced a refund policy for warehouse fulfillment errors.

One Million Coffee · Nov 2019 – Apr 2022

COVID-19 Pivot: First-Mover Packaged Coffee Format in Bandung

Crisis Innovation Channel Pivot Co-Founder
+

The Challenge

Co-founded and operated an iced coffee venture generating IDR 30–50 million in average monthly revenue from a physical kiosk in Bandung. When government COVID-19 road closures eliminated walk-in traffic practically overnight, the business model that had driven all revenue was no longer viable.

What I Did

  • Identified home consumption as the untapped channel — before any competitor in the local market had acted — and initiated a format pivot to 1-liter packaged ready-to-drink coffee.
  • Reformulated the product for longer shelf life and home delivery, designed packaging specifications, and onboarded packaging suppliers rapidly.
  • Activated home delivery via online marketplace (Tokopedia) and mobilized the existing 4-partner reseller network across West Java and Jakarta for local distribution.
  • Ran community collaborations (Word.Tee and others) at zero media spend to maintain brand visibility during the period when physical presence was restricted.

03

SKANT House of Perfumery · Oct 2022 – Sep 2024

Launching a Boutique Perfumery Brand From Zero

Founder Product Development AI Optimization D2C Launch
+

The Challenge

Build a credible boutique perfumery brand with zero existing infrastructure — no formulations, no supplier relationships, no brand identity, no operational systems — while keeping costs low enough to sustain a profitable mid-range price point in a competitive consumer market.

What I Did

  • Developed original perfume formulations using 100% authentic essential oils imported directly from origin countries (including fermented grape extract from Ukraine) — no mass-market fragrance bases, trained under a German-certified perfumery mentor.
  • Applied AI-driven tools to optimize supply chain decisions and reformulate ingredient sourcing, achieving a 28% reduction in production costs without compromising product quality.
  • Designed all product packaging in-house with a minimalist, premium aesthetic targeting the mid-range segment.
  • Built all operational infrastructure from scratch: financial management, production scheduling, inventory management, multi-stage QC protocol, and order fulfillment — managing a team of 4.
  • Launched on Tokopedia and Shopee with a disciplined D2C strategy, managing all customer communications and complaint resolution directly.
